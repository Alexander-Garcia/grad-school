Source of Remaining Divergence
------------------------------------------------------------
Each approach draws independent random perturbation
samples, producing different local neighborhoods.
This is inherent to LIME's sampling approach and
represents random noise, not systematic bias.

Conclusion
------------------------------------------------------------
By replicating the library's encoding (binary indicators
for categoricals, standardized values for continuous),
manual implementation of LIME matches the library closely. All
remaining differences are attributable to stochastic
sampling variance. Both implementations tell the same
story: this male, 3rd-class passenger with a cheap fare
has very low survival probability, driven primarily by
Sex, then Pclass and Fare.
